What Data We Collect

The SEKTUR Website

The SEKTUR website (sektur.com) is a static site. We do not use cookies, analytics, pixels, or any tracking technology. The only personal data we collect via the website is what you voluntarily provide through our contact form: your name, email address, and message content.

SEKTUR Applications

Each SEKTUR app has its own data practices and its own app-specific privacy policy. Some apps store all data exclusively on your device and collect nothing. Other apps may use cloud services to provide features like syncing and account management.

Please refer to the privacy policy for each specific app for details on what data it collects and how it is used. App-specific privacy policies are linked from within each app and from their respective pages on sektur.com.

If there is a conflict between this policy and an app-specific policy, the app-specific policy governs for that app.

In-App Purchases

Where SEKTUR apps offer in-app purchases, transactions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive, store, or process your payment card details. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy.

How We Use Your Data

We use personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries: Replying to messages submitted through our contact form.
  • Providing and maintaining our apps: Delivering the features and functionality of each SEKTUR application.
  • Processing purchases: Facilitating in-app purchases handled by Apple via StoreKit.
  • Legal compliance: Meeting applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Sharing Your Data

We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us deliver our services (e.g., Formspree for contact form processing, Apple for in-app purchases). These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.
  • Law enforcement when required by valid legal process, court order, or governmental regulation.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. We do not maintain formal databases of user contact information.

Device-only apps: For apps that store data exclusively on your device (e.g., Quota), we never receive or retain that data. Deleting the app deletes all data.

Cloud-connected apps: For apps that use cloud storage, your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Specific retention details are provided in each app's privacy policy.

In-app purchase records processed by Apple are subject to Apple's retention policies. We do not store payment card details.

Security of Your Data

The security of your data is important to us. For device-only apps, your data security is governed by your device's own protections (passcode, Face ID, device encryption). For cloud-connected apps and website submissions, we use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS).

No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially reasonable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Third-Party Services

The following third-party services may process data in connection with SEKTUR products:

  • Apple StoreKit — processes in-app purchases. apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions on sektur.com. formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy

Individual SEKTUR apps may use additional third-party services. These will be disclosed in each app's specific privacy policy.

Your Rights under the CCPA/CPRA

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know/Access: Request what personal data we have collected about you and why.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell or share personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Data: You may request limits on how we use sensitive personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

Exercising Your CCPA/CPRA Rights

To exercise any of your rights under the CCPA/CPRA, contact us at hello@sektur.com. Only you, or a person you authorize to act on your behalf, may make a verifiable request. We will respond within 45 days.

Your Rights — EU, UK & Swiss Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or applicable local law:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
